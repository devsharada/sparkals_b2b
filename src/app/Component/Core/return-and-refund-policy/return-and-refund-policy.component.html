Return & Refund Policy

1. Return Policy

Goods may be returned under the following conditions:

  • Returns must be initiated within 5 (five) calendar days of receiving the goods.
  • Items must be returned in their original condition, packaging, and form, with no signs of tampering or damage.
  • Returns are not accepted for:
    • Any customised job work.
    • Stones that have been set into jewellery, modified, or worked on by Sparkals or any third party.
  • Return requests must be submitted in writing to [Insert Email] and approved prior to shipping the items back.
  • Return shipping costs and insurance are the responsibility of the buyer unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2. Refund Policy

  • Once returned goods are received, we will conduct a quality and authenticity check to ensure the items meet the return criteria.
  • If the return is approved, a refund will be processed within 15 (fifteen) days of receiving and verifying the returned items.
  • Refunds will be made using the original method of payment or as credit to your business account, as mutually agreed.

3. Important Notes

  • We recommend using a trackable and insured courier service for all returns.
  • Sparkals reserves the right to refuse returns that do not meet the above conditions.
  • Any disputes or discrepancies must be raised in writing within the return window.
